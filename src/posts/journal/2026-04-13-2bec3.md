<p>I have to admit, I can carry many feelings at once. I can be entirely happy and deeply wounded at the same time. Life’s uncertainties consume me, yet death does not frighten me. Such a combination is only possible when the spirit is strong, when it is unshakable. That is the nature of someone who intends to fight until the very end and is as cynical as the king of Zion. Someone like me.</p>

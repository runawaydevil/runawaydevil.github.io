I was born in **1987** and I live in São Lourenço, a small town in southeastern Brazil. I’ve been curious about how things work for as long as I can remember, which usually meant taking things apart, asking too many questions, and getting lost in books, code, and strange ideas.

### What I Do

I work across **technology**, **education**, **research**, and **writing**. My main languages are **Python** and **Go**, and I have a soft spot for **FreeBSD** and **Linux**.

I like building useful things, exploring weird ones, and learning far more than is probably necessary. My background is a bit all over the place, but somehow it all connects: *logic, creativity, systems, people, and the endless urge to understand what is going on under the hood.*

### How I Work

I’m naturally antisocial, so quiet places, computers, books, and long stretches of focused work feel pretty normal to me. I also care a lot about making knowledge more:
- Accessible
- Less bureaucratic
- Less painful than it usually is
